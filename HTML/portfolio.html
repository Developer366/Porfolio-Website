Quiz World

This is a webapp that gives the user 2 quizes that they can choose from. The two options are Java Quiz and HTML/CSS Quiz. The user gets around 20 questions per quiz and a timer starts when a quiz is selected. Getting a question wrong shows the right answer and an explanation. At the end of the quiz, your score is shown and how long you were taking the quiz.
